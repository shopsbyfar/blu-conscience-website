Our Mission: Main Street, Not MAGA.

We are a coalition of independent business owners who believe in community, prosperity, and democracy. When authoritarian rhetoric threatens our economy and our values, we refuse to stay silent.

BluConscience provides the tools—from bulk merchandise for your storefront to a platform for unified action—to empower entrepreneurs to become a visible, formidable force for change. We're turning our storefronts into statements and our sales into support for the pro-democracy movement.
